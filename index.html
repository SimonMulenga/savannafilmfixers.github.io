Services

Check our Services

Crew and Equipment Arrangements

This service provides assistance and support in managing the production process, ensuring smooth operations for the film crew. This will involve hiring skilled crew members, such as cameramen, sound technicians, and lighting experts. And we also provide facilitate equipment rentals, ensuring that all necessary gear is available and in working condition.

Location Scouting and Permits

This service focuses on finding suitable filming locations that meet the requirements and vision of the production. It also involves researching and identify suitable filming location based on the script's requirement or brief provided to us, then we obtain necessary permits and permissions for filming in specific areas, including national parks, heritage sites, cities and any other location you may require, we will deliver to your expectations.

Transportation and Accommodation

The recruitment service helps in sourcing and selecting qualified individuals for various roles in the film production.

Casting and Extras

Provide assistance in casting actors suitable for the roles defined in the script and also be able to organize and manage extras background actors as needed.

Logistical Support

Coordinate schedules, ensuring that all team members are aware of the call times and filming sequences. We also manage the flow of production by arranging for meals, breaks, and other necessities.

Emergency Solutions

Develop contingency plans for unexpected situations such as weather disruptions or equipment failures. Have a network of local resources to quickly resolve any on-set emergencies.

Post-Production Assistance

Connect production with local Post-Production facilities and experts. Aid in arranging dubbing, submitting, and editing services if required.

Budget Management

Assist in creating and managing the production budget, ensuring that costs are controlled and transparent. Provide cost-effective solutions without compromising on production quality.

Legal and Regulatory Guidance

Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements, such as contracts and intellectual property rights. Offer advice on tax incentives and rebates available in certain filming locations.
